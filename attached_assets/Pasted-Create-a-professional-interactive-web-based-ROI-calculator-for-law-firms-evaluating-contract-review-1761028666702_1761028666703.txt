Create a professional, interactive web-based ROI calculator for law firms evaluating contract review automation. The calculator should be clean, mobile-responsive, and provide instant results with visual feedback.

Page Header
Main Heading: "Contract Review Automation ROI Calculator"

Subheading: "See exactly how much time, money, and realization your firm could gain by automating contract review—in under 60 seconds. Get board-ready numbers based on your actual workflow."

Calculator Structure & Logic
Section 1: Your Current State

Input Fields:

Number of associates performing contract reviews (number input, default: 40)

Average contracts reviewed per associate per week (number input, default: 6)

Average hours spent per contract review (number input with decimal, default: 4.2)

Blended hourly rate (currency input with $ symbol, default: $350)

Current realization rate (%) (percentage input, default: 85%)

Annual AI tool licensing cost (currency input, default: $50,000)

Section 2: Expected Improvement (Pre-filled Conservative Estimates)

Input Fields (with helper text explaining the conservative benchmark):
7. Expected time reduction (%) (percentage slider, range: 20-50%, default: 30%, helper text: "Industry benchmarks show 30-40% time savings for mid-sized firms")
8. Expected realization rate improvement (%) (percentage input, range: 0-10%, default: 3%, helper text: "Faster turnaround and fewer write-downs typically lift realization 2-5%")

Calculation Logic
Step 1: Calculate Annual Baseline Metrics

Total contracts reviewed annually = (Number of associates) × (Contracts per week) × 52 weeks

Total hours spent annually = (Total contracts) × (Hours per contract)

Total billable value = (Total hours) × (Blended hourly rate)

Realized revenue (current) = (Total billable value) × (Current realization rate ÷ 100)

Step 2: Calculate Post-Automation Metrics

Time saved per contract = (Hours per contract) × (Time reduction % ÷ 100)

New time per contract = (Hours per contract) - (Time saved per contract)

Total hours saved annually = (Total contracts) × (Time saved per contract)

Hours reinvested in billable work = (Total hours saved) × 0.65 (conservative assumption: 65% of saved time converts to billable)

New realization rate = (Current realization rate) + (Realization improvement %)

Post-automation realized revenue = (Total billable value) × (New realization rate ÷ 100)

Step 3: Calculate ROI Metrics

Annual time savings value = (Hours reinvested) × (Blended hourly rate)

Annual realization improvement value = (Post-automation realized revenue) - (Realized revenue current)

Total annual benefit = (Annual time savings value) + (Annual realization improvement value)

Net annual benefit = (Total annual benefit) - (Annual AI tool cost)

ROI % = ((Net annual benefit) ÷ (Annual AI tool cost)) × 100

Payback period (months) = ((Annual AI tool cost) ÷ (Total annual benefit)) × 12

3-year cumulative benefit = ((Total annual benefit) × 3) - (Annual AI tool cost × 3)
